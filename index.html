The process of buying property in Dubai is relatively simple. Once you have found the right property for you and are ready to purchase, there are four main legal steps to follow:

1. Conclusion of the agreement between the buyer and the seller
2. Signing the sales contract
3. Application for a non-objection certificate
4. Carrying out the transfer of ownership with DLD
